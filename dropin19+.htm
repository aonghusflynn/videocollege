COURSES AVAILABLE 18+

“I started out on the basics and
I’ve just gone on and on learning more.”

Intoduction to Video [Download PDF]
A short, hands-on course for people with little or no experience in video production. It’s a first step that could lead to a City and Guilds 7501 Diploma in Media techniques, accredited by the National Open College Network (NOCN).

City and Guilds 7501 Diploma in Media Techniques [Download PDF]
After covering all areas of production, you’ll make six productions in the following areas and of the following durations: News (3-mins. Individually), Creative (5/10-mins. Individually), Studio (half hour magazine-type programme. (Collectively), 3 radio recordings, Music recording (3/5-mins), a Jingle (30-secs) and writing and recording a 5-min drama. These pieces of work will be assessed by the City and Guilds moderator, together with your production paperwork.

Combined Production Course [Download PDF]
This offers a 6-month, part-time course in:

  • Camera/lighting
  • Sound recording for video
  • Drama directing
  • Production management
  • Non-linear video editing
