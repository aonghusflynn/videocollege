COMMUNITY SCREENINGS

To publicise community productions, and the issues they raise, we hold screenings in local venues, such as youth clubs, schools, local cinemas, professional screening studios and community centres.

Some videos are in public places such as outpatients’ departments, sports centres, housing association foyers and local authority conferences. Others are used by the health service for training or by teachers as part of their pupils’ social education. We also distribute videos to libraries, and submit them to festivals and broadcast channels.

Wellbeing in Childbirth

An information film for the local Mental Health Trust and Arabic-speaking Community Organisations.

With funding from Kensington Housing Trust, the Primary Care Trust and Sure Start, the film was comissioned by the Mental Health Trust, with a steering group of Moroccan, Sudanese, Somali and Iraqi organisations. The film (in Arabic) was aimed at Arabic-speaking women, who were experiencing peri-natal depression, to encourage take up of local support services.

Childcare Choices

An information film for Paddington Development Trust, this community film identified opportunities available for childcare in the Paddington and Queen's Park areas.
