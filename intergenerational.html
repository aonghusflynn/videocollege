INTERGENERATIONAL PROJECTS

With Sixty Plus, now Age UK Kensington & Chelsea, a charity for older people, we have worked on three inter-generational projects.

Guns, Knives and Feeling Safe

A project with Hammersmith & Fulham Youth Offending Team (YOT) involved 16/17 year olds working with local residents to examine the effects of crime. Part of Heritage Lottery-funded, Home Roots.

For more Home Roots films

You’re Safe, I’m Safe

On this project about community safety, young and older peoples’ perceptions of crime and fear of crime were surprisingly similar. ‘You’re Safe, I’m Safe’ was screened in a local community centre with a panel of councillors and council staff. In response to young people’s ideas presented in the film, new lighting was installed throughout their housing estate

World's End estate

Ideas from residents, young and old, about how to make the World’s End estate a better place to live. Youth Offending Teams
