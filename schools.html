SCHOOL INCLUSION

One of the problems schools faced was the increase in truanting and under- achievement. For several years we worked alongside schools’ Inclusion Projects and Pupil Referral Units involving pupils at risk from exclusion or self-exclusion. We did this by encouraging these vulnerable students to explore issues such as bunking off, bullying, conflict between different groups, peer pressure and changes at school.

We found that increasing pupils’ skill levels and their confidence to work with their peers, helped them to approach their education with a different attitude.
