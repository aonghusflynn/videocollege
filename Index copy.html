The Video College was set up through a local residents' association in 1994 to give people of all ages an opportunity to train in one of the most vital areas of film making.

PROJECTS FOR 12 +

At the moment a group of 12 – 16 yr olds are finishing editing music videos, short dramas and documentaries that they have written and produced.  A new project starts 17 May with  4 days of workshops at half-term, 31 May – 3 June.

Exciting filmmaking programme for 12 - 19 year olds. For details >> Projects for 12+

SECOND LIGHT

The Video College’s Second Light programme, a First Light initiative funded by UK Film Council and Skillset has drawn to a close.
Of the eleven 18-23 year olds from London's Black and Asian communities who joined the year-long programme of film training, EIGHT have found work in the industry

For a trailer, information about the programme, trainees’ progress and the Second Light screening at the Curzon Soho >> News
