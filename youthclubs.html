YOUNG PEOPLE AND PARTNERSHIP PROGRAMMES

We worked in partnership with youth clubs, schools and youth offending teams, migrant and refugee organisations and travellers’ groups, young people with disabilities, young carers and teenage parents. We drew on our pool of skilled video and drama tutors to choose those with the right experience for each new project.

As with all our work, we encouraged young people to draw on their own experience in producing thoughtful and original films that told stories of what really matters to people moving from childhood to adulthood - and were rarely seen on television or mainstream film.
