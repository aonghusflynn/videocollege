COMMUNITY PARTNERSHIPS AND PRODUCTIONS

This strand of our programme divided into two areas, both of which shared a common aim: to provide a service for the local community, voluntary and statutory organisations.

We provided professionally produced videos in partnership with, or commissioned by, local organisations.

Working closely with our partners, and involving staff and users at each stage of production, we made a variety of videos for schools, community, voluntary and

statutory organisations for use as an educational or information tool, or to promote local services, particularly health services.

All productions were supervised by experienced producer/directors and provided excellent opportunities for our graduating trainees to gain employment and professional experience.

We also provided our production-based training programme for people of all ages, often working together, from our target groups.
