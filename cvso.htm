COMMUNITY, VOLUNTARY AND STATUTORY ORGANISATIONS

Our experience is that voluntary and statutory agencies, and local organisations are increasingly using video to support and develop their work. We have worked in partnership with many such groups to help them reach new users and publicise their services. Our videos are also used for training or informing staff, parents and carers.

We’re regularly approached by local migrant and refugee community organisations and departments of Primary Care Trusts, each of whom want to encourage more people from minority communities to take up their services or to disseminate information. Videos in different languages can reach people who neither read nor easily understand English and we are especially well placed to help as our own students are often from these communities.

With these projects we take the role of executive producer and employ trainees as crew or assistants to experienced professionals.
