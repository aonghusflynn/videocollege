FILMS AND TRAILERS

These are a sample of the different strands of our work. More can be found on other pages of our website.

 

Magpie (12’51”) written by 19 year old Kaysi Foster, directed by Mawaan Rizwan and starring Emma Carryl.

St John's Organ Project (08'16"). St John's Notting Hill received Heritage Lottery funding towards the restoration of the church's 18th century organ. 19 young people with The Video College made a short film that documents the long and intricate process of restoring the organ.

Death by Pineapple? (04'28"). A comedy shot in a day on the SECOND LIGHT training programme.

Round and Round (11'41") ‘Alex is on probation and trying to go straight; he’s working in a bike shop and saving up for his dream bike. His friend, Tee, thinks he’s a fool, and takes it for a joy ride behind Alex's back. The bike is stolen and Alex knows he will get the blame .’

Made by 16-18 year olds from West London, Round and Round was nominated for a Best Screenplay award, sponsored by EON Productions, and was created in association with First Light Movies and the UK Film Council.
