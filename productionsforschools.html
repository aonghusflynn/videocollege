PRODUCTIONS FOR SCHOOLS


For several years we’ve been making videos for use in schools.
We’ve covered a variety of issues ranging from race to graffiti to housing and homelessness. These videos are used in schools as a trigger for discussion, often as part of the PSHCE curriculum.
