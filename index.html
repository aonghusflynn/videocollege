The Video College was set up through a local residents' association in 1994 to give people of all ages an opportunity to train in one of the most vital areas of film making.

Find out more about us>>

We are no longer at our studio and office space at 16 Telford Road, but have funding from the Royal Borough of Kensington & Chelsea until the end of January 2016.

At 2pm on 15th January, we'll be running our second Film Club screening at the Venture Centre, 103a Wornington Road, W10 6LS.

We're also making a film about the Venture Centre and welcome 12-15 year olds to take part in the filming on Saturdays 6th & 20th February from 11am-4pm.
