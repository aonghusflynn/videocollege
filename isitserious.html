IS IT SERIOUS?

“It’s hands-on”

For 12+ How do you know if you want to do something if you’ve never tried it before? You’ll work in small groups with experienced tutors and have the chance to turn your ideas into actual videos. Though making a really good video needs you to be committed, you’ll also find it’s fun. To find out what’s going on >> Projects for 12+.

For 18+ It doesn’t matter if your education got cut short, or you’re unemployed, or you don’t have the money for further education. All you need is a burning desire to learn about video and we’ll do all we can to help you achieve that.

Our training programme is no free lunch; the digital video, television and film industries are a hard place to work. We’re looking for people who are prepared to give a 100%. There are a lot of applicants for every single job and it takes a genuine sense of “I really want to do that”, to get on.

We’ll do all we can to help you, by offering industry-recognised training from basic video skills to support in getting into work. All our courses are taught by professionals in the field and are free to selected applicants.

To find out what courses are available >> Training for 18+.
