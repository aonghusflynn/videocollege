TRAINING FOR 18 +

"I started out on the basics and I've just gone on and on learning more"

We offered industry standard training in digital video production. We ran courses, with a ratio of eight to ten students to two or more tutors, better than most colleges.

All our courses were free. We helped with childcare costs, our training studio and offices were wheelchair accessible and we did everything we could to help people with any particular needs.
