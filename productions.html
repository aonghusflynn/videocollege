PARTNERSHIP AND COMMISSIONED PRODUCTIONS

This strand provides professionally produced videos in partnership with, or commissioned by, local organisations.

Working closely with our partners, and involving staff and users at each stage of production, we’ve made a variety of videos for schools, community, voluntary and statutory organisations for use as an educational or information tool, or to promote local services, particularly health services.

All productions are supervised by experienced producer/directors and provide excellent opportunities for our graduating trainees to gain employment and professional experience.
